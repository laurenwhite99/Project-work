Prawn Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of green beans
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1 tin of sweetcorn
  • 150g king prawns
  • 1 sachet of soy sauce
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 lime
  • 2 packs of noodles

Equipment

  • Chopping board
  • Knife
  • Saucepan
  • Sieve
  • Zester
  • Frying pan

Cooking Time

20 minutes

Nutrition Facts

  • Energy (kJ/kCal) - 1703/407
  • Fat (g) - 4
  • Carbohydrate (g) - 68
  • Sugars (g) - 15
  • Protein (g) - 25
  • Salt (g) - 6.2

Cooking Instructions

  1. Bring a large pan of water to the boil with 0.5 tsp of salt for the noodles. Trim the green beans then chop in half. Trim the spring onions then slice thinly. Peel and grate the garlic (or use a garlic press). Zest the lime and cut into wedges. Drain the sweetcorn in a sieve.
  2. When the saucepan of water is boiling, add the noodles and cook until tender, 4 mins. Drain in a sieve.
    3. >
  3. While the noodles are cooking, heat a drizzle of oil in a large frying pan on high heat. Once hot, add the green beans and stir fry for 2 mins.
  4. Add the prawns and sweetcorn and continue stir frying for 3-4 mins, until the beans are tender and the prawns are just cooked. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. IMPORTANT: The prawns are cooked when pink on the outside and opaque in the middle.
  5. Add the pad thai paste, soy sauce, half the lime zest and the noodles to the pan and mix well. Add a splash of water to loosen if you need to. Cook until the noodles are piping hot.
  6. Divide the pad thai between plates and top with the spring onion, remaining lime zest and lime wedges. Enjoy!
